Project Purpose

The AI Instructional Effectiveness Project aims to harness the power of artificial intelligence and data analysis to evaluate and enhance instructional materials and methods. We aim to enhance the learning experience for students by providing an easy to use A.I Web Portal where instructors can upload audio and video recordings of lectures to be transcribed, analyzed, and visualized. Our project will aim to feature a question classification system, speaker diarization, a correctable and downloadable transcript, and easy to follow data visualization that will react to your corrections.

Deliverables

  • Data Collection and Analysis Framework
  • Machine Learning Models for Assessment
  • Improved Instructional Recommendations
  • Evaluation Reports and Documentation
